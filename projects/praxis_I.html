Praxis I Project: Plant Humidifier

I was never a big fan of the content (e.g. models, design principals) taught in engineering design classes like Praxis since I found them often to be very impractical. The only model I ever really used out of all the models they taught in class was the requirements model.

However, I really enjoyed the group work in Praxis. For our final project, my group worked on a design to keep the engineering science plant mascot from withering away due to lack of water. After multiples iterations and testing, we settled on a design that used an ultrasonic humidifier to keep the soil at the right moisture level.
