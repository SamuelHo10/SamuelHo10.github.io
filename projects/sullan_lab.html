Over the summer, I developed a Python application utilizing the Tkinter library for the user interface and the Pandas library for data manipulation. The application is designed to analyze AFM single-cell force spectroscopy data by filtering out outliers, generating visual graphs, and formatting data into a comprehensible spreadsheet. The source code for the application is available here.

Furthermore, I worked with a third year chemistry student on their to a microfluidics project aimed at creating a device coated with a metal catalyst to enhance the degradation of methylene blue. The device was fabricated by casting PDMS, applying a PDA coating, and then a final metal layer. Characterization of the metal coating was performed using Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) to assess adhesion quality. The device's efficacy was tested using a microplate UV-Vis reader to measure the concentration of methylene blue before and after passing through the device.
