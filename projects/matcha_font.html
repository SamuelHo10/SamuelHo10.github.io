APS360 Final Project: Matcha Font

In APS360, Applied Fundamentals of Deep Learning, my group created a font detection AI for the final project. No one in my group had sufficient computing equipment, so the model took ages to train. We spent weeks trying to pre-train an autoencoder to implement it into our model. However, no matter what we tried, it always performed worse than our baseline CNN. For the final model, we ended up simply increasing the size of our CNN and included normalization and dropout within our layers. We learned that often times simpler models work better than complicated ones.

More information about the project can be found in our final report.
