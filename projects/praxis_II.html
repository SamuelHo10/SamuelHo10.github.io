Praxis II Project: Worm Wheel 360

For Praxis II, our group was assigned to Allan Gardens with the objective of minimizing the effort required for vermicomposting (composting with worms). We designed a large rotating wheel system that sifted out the compost while retaining the worms, incorporating a lid to prevent the need for worm transfer. We dedicated substantial time to this project, prioritizing it over other commitments in the final weeks to focus on the presentation and prototype development. We also invested approximately $300 of our own funds in building materials. Despite our extensive efforts, the final presentation received a grade of 75% from the teaching assistants due to our lack of rehearsal for the interview questions 😭.
