Praxis II Project: Worm Wheel 360

For Praxis II, our group was assigned to the Allan Gardens community with the objective of minimizing the effort required for vermicomposting (composting with worms). We designed a large rotating wheel system that sifted out the compost while retaining the worms, incorporating a lid to prevent the need for worm transfer. We prioritized this project in the final weeks, focusing on the presentation and prototype, and invested around $300 of personal funds in materials. Despite our extensive efforts, our final presentation received a grade of 75% from the teaching assistants (probably cause of our lack of rehearsal for the interview questions). I am sad 😭.
